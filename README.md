# Healthcare
<h1>AI-DRIVEN PUBLIC HEALTHCARE CHATBOT FOR DISEASE AWARENESS  </h1>
<h2>Description:</h2>
Create a multilingual AI chatbot to educate rural and semi-urban populations about preventive healthcare, disease symptoms, and vaccination schedules. The chatbot should integrate with government health databases and provide real-time alerts for outbreaks.

<h2>Expected Outcome: </h2>
A chatbot accessible via WhatsApp or SMS, reaching 80% accuracy in answering health queries and increasing awareness by 20% in target communities.

<h2>Technical Feasibility</h2>
Built using NLP frameworks (e.g., Rasa, Dialogflow) with APIs for health data integration, deployable on cloud platforms for scalability.

<h1> Now Let's Understand it in details  </h1>
<h2>Detailed explanation of Problem Statement</h2>
<ul>
<li>Large parts of rural and semi-urban populations lack timely, localized disease information.</li>
<li>Fragmented communication between people and healthcare departments leads to missed vaccinations and delayed reporting. </li>
<li>Language barriers + low-bandwidth devices prevent many users from accessing digital health guidance.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Proposed Solution:</h2>
<ul><li>We have designed an AI chatbot which has NLP capabilities and it is easily/freely accessible in our Website/WhatsApp/SMS</li></ul>
• Our chatbot services are :-
<ol>
<li>Multilingual conversation for all health-related queries</li>
<li>Symptoms check and preventive tips</li>
<li>Vaccination details and alert through WhatsApp/SMS</li>
<li>Advanced seasonal healthcare alerts accordance with location</li>
<li>Good nutrition and hygiene tips to all</li>
<li>Interactive activity (like games , comics , videos etc )sessions for awareness to all</li>
categories
<li>Provides traceability to government against infectious disease and also about health conditions in their locality.</li>
</ol>
<b>In future we can add Agentic AI capabilities to it so that an Agent can develop which can do book vaccination slots , book doctor appointments etc.. </b>
